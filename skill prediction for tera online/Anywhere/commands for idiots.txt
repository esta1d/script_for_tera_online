This is a quick overview of all available commands:

bank			Opens the Banker.
gbank			Opens the Guild Bank.
petbank			Opens the Special Storage (bank from pets).
wardrobe		Opens the Wardrobe.
angler			Opens the Angler Token Vendor
fishsup			Opens the Fishing Supplies.
merc		    Opens the Merchant.
magsup		    Opens the Magic Materials.
crystal			Opens the Crystal Merchant.
spec		    Opens the Specialty Store.
vgsup			Opens the Vanguard Initiative Quartermaster.
vgcrys			Opens the Vanguard Initiative Crystal Merchant.
bellic		    Opens the Bellicarium Quartermaster.
hustler			Opens the Hustler.
elleon			Opens the Elleon Token Store.
guardian		Opens the Guardian Legion Credit Store.
mmv			    Opens the Mystery Merchant in Velika.
mmh			    Opens the Mystery Merchant in Highwatch.
mmk			    Opens the Mystery Merchant in Kaiator.
mma			    Opens the Mystery Merchant in Allemantheia.
